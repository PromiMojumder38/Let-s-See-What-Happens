At first this Mathematics club used to have a different name ‘Own Mathematical inventions’. The idea behind opening a Facebook group with that name was encouraging people to solve mathematical problems in their own way, rather than how they were taught. It wouldn’t be a crime to call those methods their very own ‘Mathematical inventions’. At first, only one person used to take care of that group, who was Parthib Mojumdar. He also took exams with a bit of convoluted questions. Another boy happened to attend one of the exams, namely Debapriya Saha Roy. After that, these two became very good friends and soon Debapriya became one of the admins of that group. Debapriya opened a page for ‘Own Mathematical inventions’. Another friend of Parthib was Tribani Paul Roshmi, who also was really passionate about mathematics, joined as an admin. At the same time Debapriya opened a messenger group called ‘Math Enthusiasts’ (currently inactive) and there they met Ashraful Islam Fahim, Champion of the Josephite Math Mania, and he attended as an admin soon after. As soon as, Fahim got connected, he took the club activities to the next level. (Ashraful Islam Fahim was one of the six members of International Mathematical Olympiad team of Bangladesh) But suddenly they thought that the club activities had gone way past just inventing mathematical methods, So they named it ‘Miracles of Math’. Then, a passionate admin Ali Hussain Md. Onkon came. At the event of 16th December,2020 they decided to make a special question, while they were thinking about it, Mahdi Ahmed Mahi made an amazing question and he became an admin too. On the 2nd anniversary of the club, a big event was organized, a lot of segments were included there for the participants. Then Shah Ahmed Jubaer, Khandoker Naeemul Haque, Rahul Paul, Nafis Al Zahid and Md. Nafis Noor Taseen joined the club as a admin and they started to get their act together and ‘Miracles of Math’ reached the spot it has now. A lot of members are now working on a variety of tasks,including creating questions,answers,results,organizing various contests and much more work. Our slogan is "Excursionist of mathematics." It means we cordially invited all of you to join with the journey of mathematics and gain more and more knowledge about mathematics! Activities: • Weekly Olympiads • Contest • Live sessions and discussion • Note Sharing • Mentoring
